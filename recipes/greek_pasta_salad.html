Greek Pasta Salad

Description

I really like this recipe because it's cheap and hella good

Ingredients

  • Rotini
  • Black Olives
  • Feta Cheese
  • Kidney Beans
  • 1 Shallot
  • Dill, Basil, and Oregano seasoning

    • Dressing

  • Olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Tahini or mayo
  • Honey
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. 1. Boil water
  2. 2. While water is boiling cut shallot into thin slices
  3. 3. Add pasta to boiling water
  4. 4. Create Dressing using the ingredients under dressing
  5. 5. Drain pasta
  6. 6. Cook shallots in olive oil
  7. 7. Add pasta, dressing, olives, kidney beans, and feta
  8. 8. Stir and enjoy warm or cold
